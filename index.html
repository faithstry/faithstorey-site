No. 02 / About

Get To Know Me

I'm a thirty-something nomad based in Austin. In my past life, I was a marketer based in Hawaii who mostly worked in tech. While tech is great and helps change the world, I realized I wanted something more.

I'm a person who gets bored very easily so I constantly change up projects, where I live, and the way I think about life. So now I'm growing my Airbnb business and actively looking to buy a business and grow a holding company. I fell in love with design in the process so now I do interior design on the side. One could say I'm sidequestmaxxing.

Outside of my work life, I LOVE to travel, and plan to see the majority of the world in my short span of time on this planet. I also enjoy hiking, fitness, and editing silly little vlogs. If you want to work with me, reach out below.
